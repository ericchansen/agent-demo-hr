# Demo personas

Two synthetic HR business partners, each scoped to their region. Swap between
them with the `ROSTER_DEV_UPN` env var to demonstrate row-level security.

| Persona | UPN | Scope (`hr_access`) |
|---------|-----|---------------------|
| EMEA HR Business Partner | `emea.hrbp@contoso.com` | `allowed_region = EMEA` (all teams) |
| APAC HR Business Partner | `apac.hrbp@contoso.com` | `allowed_region = APAC` (all teams) |

Both ask the identical question ("export the active roster for my region") and
get **different rows** — EMEA ~1088 active, APAC ~822 active. Scope is enforced
server-side by every Roster MCP tool; the persona can never widen it.

> Fictional identities for a fictional company. The scope map lives in
> `data/hr_access.csv` (generated by `data/generate_hr.py`).
